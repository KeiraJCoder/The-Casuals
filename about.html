Meet The Band



Laura
Jarvis

Laura Jarvis is a seasoned singer with an extensive background performing in social clubs and holiday sites. Over the years, her melodious voice has graced many stages, including being a key member of an Abba tribute band. Laura's remarkable journey in the world of music also led her to be the voice behind the Casuals. Through her songs and performances, she continues to captivate and inspire audiences.

Donald
Campbell

Donald Campbell is a gifted keyboard and piano player whose passion for music resonates both on and off the stage. When he's not delighting audiences as the keyboard player for the Casuals, Donald dedicates his time to tutoring, sharing his profound knowledge and expertise. His dual role as a performer and educator showcases his commitment to music and his love for sharing it with others.

John
MacGillivray

John MacGillivray's guitar skills are a force to be reckoned with. As the talented guitar player for the band, his strings bring melodies to life and set the tone for unforgettable performances. John's passion for music is evident in every chord and note he plays, captivating listeners with his artistry and dedication to his craft.

John
McLelland

John McLelland's prowess behind the drums is unmistakable. As the heartbeat of the band, he ensures that every performance is rhythmically on point. Outside of his drumming, John is a dedicated fan of the Stone Roses, with their influence palpable in his style and approach to music. His love for the iconic band adds depth to his musical journey and inspires him to continue honing his skills.
